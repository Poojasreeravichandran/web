HEERAMANDI

Sanjay Leela Bhansali
Release date 1 May 2024 (India)
Hindustani
The series is about the lives of tawaifs in the red-light district of Heera Mandi in Lahore
during the Indian independence movement against the British Raj.
It stars Manisha Koirala, Sonakshi Sinha, Aditi Rao Hydari, Richa Chadha and Taha Shah Badussha.
